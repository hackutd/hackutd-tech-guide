## About the Company

NorthMark Compute & Cloud (NMC²) is a new force in high performance data infrastructure. Backed by dedicated leadership and investment, NMC² has a clear mission as it works at the bleeding edge of technology: to scale and enhance the High Performance Compute (HPC) and cloud infrastructure that underpins its clients’ research, production and delivery of the means to reach breakthroughs in shaping the industries of tomorrow. The company partners with forward-thinking enterprises seeking to accelerate innovation in engineering, advanced analytics, and data-driven decision-making. By removing infrastructure bottlenecks and delivering massive computational power, NMC² enables businesses to solve large-scale engineering challenges, optimize complex systems, and uncover insights from vast datasets. Their expert team provides scalable, high-performance environments tailored to organizations aiming to lead in innovation and operational efficiency. Whether running large simulations or training powerful AI models, NMC² delivers the speed, capacity, and reliability needed to stay ahead in today’s competitive landscape.
