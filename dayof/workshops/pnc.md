# AI-Powered Product Management: A Day in the Life, Reimagined

**Company:** PNC

**Time:** Saturday, 1:00pm - 1:45pm

**Location:** ECSW 1.315

---

## Workshop Description

**Reimagining Product Management with Generative AI**

- Explore the Product Manager's lifecycle and identify key pain points in strategy, development, and delivery.
- Discover how Generative AI can accelerate decision-making and streamline workflows across ideation, research, and execution.
- Learn practical use cases and tools for automating repetitive tasks like backlog grooming, sprint planning, and reporting.
- Gain insights into building AI-driven solutions that enhance collaboration and focus on high-value outcomes.
