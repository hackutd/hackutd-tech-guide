# Devpost Guide (Markdown)

**Devpost:** [hackutd-2025.devpost.com](https://hackutd-2025.devpost.com/)

Two deadlines, one platform. Register your team early, then polish your submission before judging — without getting disqualified on a technicality.

<div className="margin-top--md margin-bottom--lg">
  <a href="https://hackutd-2025.devpost.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" className="button button--primary margin-right--md">Register Team</a>
  <a href="https://hackutd-2025.devpost.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" className="button button--secondary">Submit Project</a>
</div>

:::tip TL;DR — read these three

1. **Public repo only** — link your code on Devpost; private repos can disqualify you.
2. **Max 2 sponsor tracks** — you can still compete for general / MLH prizes.
3. **Invite every teammate** on Devpost — one submission per team; everyone must be on it for prizes.

:::

## Final Pre-flight Check

- [ ] Public GitHub repo linked on Devpost (private = risk).
- [ ] At most **2** sponsor / company tracks selected.
- [ ] All teammates invited on the project (prize eligibility).
- [ ] Demo video is public (YouTube, etc.) — aim for about **2 minutes** or less.
- [ ] You finished the **final Submit** flow — not just a saved draft.

## Pick your path

<Tabs groupId="devpost-markdown-flow" queryString defaultValue="saturday">
<TabItem value="saturday" label="Saturday: Team Registration (2 mins)">

:::danger Deadline

**Saturday 11:00 PM** — your team must be registered on Devpost (complete the final Submit step).

:::

### 1. Join the hackathon

Open the [HackUTD Devpost page](https://hackutd-2025.devpost.com/) and sign up or log in. Complete your profile when prompted.

<ExpandableImage src="/img/hackerpacks/join-hackathon.png" alt="Join Hackathon button" caption="Join Hackathon button" />

<ExpandableImage src="/img/hackerpacks/devpost-sign-up.png" alt="Devpost sign up or log in" caption="Devpost sign up or log in" />

<ExpandableImage src="/img/hackerpacks/registration-form.png" alt="Registration form" caption="Registration form" />

### 2. Create a project (blank is OK)

Go to **My Projects → Create Project**. For registration only, use your team name or “Team Registration” and placeholders where needed — you can edit before the final deadline.

<ExpandableImage src="/img/hackerpacks/create-project.png" alt="Create Project" caption="Create Project" />

### 3. Invite your whole team

**Manage Team** → invite everyone (the email tied to their Devpost account).

<ExpandableImage src="/img/hackerpacks/manage-team.png" alt="Manage Team" caption="Manage Team" />

### 4. Project overview (registration)

Title can be your team name or “Team Registration”; elevator pitch can list teammates or a short idea.

<ExpandableImage src="/img/hackerpacks/project-overview.png" alt="Project Overview" caption="Project Overview" />

### 5. Submit through the final screen

Pick sponsor tracks you’re **considering** (max **two** company/sponsor tracks). Placeholder video/repo is OK until Sunday.

**You must complete the final Submit step** or you are **not** registered.

</TabItem>
<TabItem value="sunday" label="Sunday: Final Submission (10 mins)">

:::danger Deadline

**Sunday 12:00 PM (noon)** — final project due on Devpost.

:::

### 1. Open your project for editing

**My Projects** → **Edit Project**. You can keep editing until the hack ends.

<ExpandableImage src="/img/hackerpacks/my-projects.png" alt="My Projects" caption="My Projects" />

### 2. Project Overview

Title, pitch, thumbnail (recommended), technologies, links.

<ExpandableImage src="/img/hackerpacks/project-overview-full.png" alt="Project Overview (full)" caption="Project Overview (full)" />

### 3. Project Details (Markdown)

Describe what you built, how to run it, demo-ready vs. prototype. Add screenshots or GIFs.

<ExpandableImage src="/img/hackerpacks/project-details.png" alt="Project Details" caption="Project Details" />

<ExpandableImage src="/img/hackerpacks/details-2.png" alt="More project details" caption="More project details" />

### 4. Additional information

Sponsor challenges (**≤2** company tracks), **public** repo, demo video, schools for eligibility.

<ExpandableImage src="/img/hackerpacks/additional-information.png" alt="Additional information" caption="Additional information" />

### 5. Agree and submit

Re-read **Final Pre-flight Check** at the top when you’re done.

## Media requirements

| What | What to know |
| --- | --- |
| **Demo video** | Public link. About **2 minutes** is ideal. |
| **Thumbnails & screenshots** | Show what actually works. |
| **GitHub URL** | **Public** repo only — private is a disqualification risk. |

---

:::warning Don't forget (safety net)

- **Never leave the repo private.**
- **School emails / profiles** — match organiser rules for eligibility.
- **One submission per team** — no duplicate projects.

:::

## Prevent disqualification

- Submit early; keep editing until the Devpost cutoff.
- Invite issues → verify Devpost email, resend.
- Devpost bugs → another browser / cache clear → ask staff.
